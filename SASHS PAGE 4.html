What is HUMSS?

HUMSS stands for Humanities and Social Sciences. It's an academic strand or track in senior high school (SHS) in the Philippines, under the K-12 education system. HUMSS focuses on: *Humanities:* 1. Languages (English, Filipino, etc.) 2. History 3. Philosophy 4. Arts (music, theater, visual arts) 5. Literature *Social Sciences:* 1. Psychology 2. Sociology 3. Anthropology 4. Economics 5. Political Science

Why join HUMSS?

HUMSS aims to: 1. Develop critical thinking and analytical skills 2. Foster empathy, cultural understanding, and social responsibility 3. Prepare students for diverse careers 4. Encourage creative expression and problem-solving 5. Cultivate effective communication and collaboration skills
