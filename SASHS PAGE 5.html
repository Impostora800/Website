SASHS TVL

What is TVL?

The Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) track is designed for students who wish to gain practical and job-ready skills. It covers various specializations such as cookery, electronics, and caregiving, among others.

Why choose TVL?

TVL provides hands-on training and certification that makes students ready for employment after senior high school. It is ideal for those who want to enter the workforce or start their businesses.
